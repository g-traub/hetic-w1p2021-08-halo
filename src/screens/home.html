Moving :
In turns, each player chooses either to move his pawn or to pose one of his barriers.
The player moves one case at a time, horizontally or vertically, forward or backwards ; the barriers block the player. Click your player, then the highlighted tile you want to move to.

Barriers :
A barrier is 2 tiles long.
It must be placed between tiles.
The laying of barriers can help the player to build his own path or to slow down his opponent. It's forbidden to completely block the other player.

When the two players are in front of each other, it's possible to jump over the other player.

Goal :
Reach the other side of the map before the other player to win!
